## Design Patterns to Consider

- **Calendar View:** Grid showing entries with visual indicators (dots, colors, mood emojis), jump to specific dates
- **Entry List:** Chronological list with entry preview text, date headers, mood/category badges
- **Writing Mode:** Distraction-free full-width text area with minimal UI (only toolbar when needed)
- **Prompts System:** "What's on your mind?" placeholder text that transforms to lightbulb suggestion button if user hesitates
- **Rich Formatting:** Bold, italic, underline, headings, bullet lists with clean toolbar that auto-hides
- **Media Integration:** Simple image/photo upload capability with thumbnail previews in entry

## Color/Style Notes

- **Writing Surface:** White or very light gray background with clear, readable font (serif for headers, sans-serif for body)
- **Accent Colors:** Use soft, muted colors for mood indicators rather than bright primary colors
- **Calendar:** Light grid with subtle date numbers; highlight entry days with soft color wash
- **Typography:** Large (18-20px) body text for readability; use line-height of 1.6+ for comfort
- **Entry List:** Add subtle left accent bar in mood color to identify entry sentiment at a glance
- **Spacing:** Comfortable paragraph spacing (1.5em+), generous margins around writing area
