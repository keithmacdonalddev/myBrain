Summary: Selective Glass Strategy

Apply glass to chrome, keep content solid.

Component Treatment Blur Opacity
Sidebar Glass 12px 75-80%
Topbar Glass 12px 75-80%
Modal Glass (heavy) 20px 85%

Key Properties

  • backdrop-filter: blur() ‚Äî Creates the frosted glass effect
  • Semi-transparent background ‚Äî rgba() with 0.7-0.85 alpha
  • Subtle border ‚Äî rgba(255,255,255,0.1-0.2) for edge definition
  • Inset highlight ‚Äî Top edge glow for glass "shine"
  • Soft shadow ‚Äî Adds depth without harsh edges
